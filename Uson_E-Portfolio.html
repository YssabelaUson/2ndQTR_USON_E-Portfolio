1st Quarter Reflection in ICT-TLE

Time sure flies by swiftly, it's already the end of the first quarter. To be honest, ninth grade has been exhausting. All the lessons, activities, exams, and outputs got harder and our workload doubled when compared to last year. But, I am trying my best to keep up.

If I were given the chance to go back to the first day of ninth grade and give my past self a piece of advice, it would be "Do not procrastinate nor cram". One of the hardest challenges I faced this quarter was managing my time, unlike in eighth grade where I could cram and speed run all my tasks, now, I can't even sleep if there's still a pending activity on my to-do list. Also, the projects and performance tasks given to us take a lot of time and effort to finish. An example is the Interactive Notebook in Araling Panlipunan, I spent days and nights working and accomplishing that project. Another example is what you're reading and seeing right now, yes, this E-Portfolio. However, even though it takes most of my time, I still learn and progress which is a sign that my knowledge about these unfamiliar things is developing. I wasn't interested in coding back then, and that's why I failed some of my early "hands-on" in ICT. But, those failures gave me the motivation to practice more and enhance my skills in this subject.

In conclusion, ICT or ninth grade in general has been full of ups and downs this quarter. Now, I know what to focus on more in the next quarters to make up for my mistakes. That's all for my reflection, good luck to my classmates, batchmates, and especially myself.

2nd Quarter Reflection in ICT-TLE

Throughout this quarter of learning CSS, my skills and knowledge have developed on how to style and organize my web pages. This is thanks to our ICT teacher Ms. Uminga, for always giving us the chance to explore coding on our own. However there are times when I'm under a lot of pressure, specifically time pressure during hands-on.

To be honest, we didn't have a lot of time to discuss our lessons in ICT this quarter. Due to extracurriculars and events held every month by other subjects. But, we still managed to accomplish some tasks assigned to us. I'm also thankful that our teacher is open to giving us time for other activities, like the MVP, VPOP, and many more.

Additionally, coding with CSS has helped me maintain cleanliness in my web page, by providing various layouts that are easy to understand. I hope that I can learn more about coding next quarter. Good luck to all!
